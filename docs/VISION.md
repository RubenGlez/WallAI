# Vision

## What SprayDeck is

SprayDeck is a digital workbench for street artists and graffiti writers. It puts a professional spray paint color catalog, a palette builder, and a wall simulation tool in one offline-first mobile app.

The app is built by a solo developer in spare time and will always be free. There is no backend, no accounts, no subscriptions.

## Who it's for

**Primary user:** an active graffiti writer or street artist who:
- Plans pieces before hitting the wall
- Works with multiple spray paint brands and needs to compare colors across them
- Shoots reference photos and wants to translate them into a spray paint palette
- Wants to visualize how a design will look on a specific wall before committing

**Secondary user:** someone getting started in graffiti who needs a color reference and doesn't yet know the catalog well.

## The core problem

Spray paint color management is fragmented. Each brand has its own physical chart, there's no standard way to compare colors across brands, and there's no digital tool purpose-built for writers. Planning a piece today means carrying physical swatches, eyeballing color matches, and guessing how a design will look on a rough wall.

## What makes it different

1. **Cross-brand color matching** — LAB color space comparison lets users find the closest equivalent to any color across all brands in the catalog. This is the key differentiator.
2. **Photo-to-palette import** — extract dominant colors from a reference image and get the nearest spray paint matches instantly.
3. **Wall simulator** — overlay a sketch on a wall photo with full gesture control and opacity, so the artist can visualize the final piece in situ.
4. **Fully offline** — no account, no connectivity needed. The app works anywhere.

## Guiding principles

- **Offline first.** No backend, no auth, no network calls. Everything lives on the device.
- **Android first.** iOS is out of scope for the current version.
- **Free, always.** Monetization is through voluntary support (Ko-fi), not paywalls or ads.
- **Lean and focused.** No features that require infrastructure. Complexity only where it earns its keep.
- **Dark by default.** The UI is designed for low-light environments where artists often work.
